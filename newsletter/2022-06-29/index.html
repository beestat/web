Air Quality Has Arrived!

As soon as ecobee officially released their new thermostat with air quality, I knew it wouldn’t be long before someone asked to see the data in beestat. Not being one to disappoint, I started on it immediately. Beestat is currently the only place where you can see this historical data; not even the ecobee app has it! Check it out and please let me know your thoughts!
