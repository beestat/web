Privacy

By using beestat, you agree to allow the collection and processing of the information described in this privacy statement.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your ecobee account, beestat initiates a sync of nearly all of the accessible data to a local database. This includes, but is not limited to, these things:

  • Email address
  • Physical address and home characteristics
  • Thermostat and sensor listings
  • Thermostat configuration and schedule
  • Historical runtime data

2. How We Use Information

All of the collected information is used to provide a service to you. This includes all of the normal application functionality. Additionally, your information will be used in aggregate to provide anonymous home comparisons to other users.

3. How We Share Information

In order to provide a meaningful service, the following third party services receive parts of your data. This includes things like your email address, physical address, and browser information. Third parties never receive more information than necessary.

  • Digital Ocean
  • Mailchimp
  • SmartyStreets
  • Sentry

4. Data Retention

High resolution (5 minute) runtime data is stored for approximately one year. All other information is stored indefinitely. If you would like to have your data removed, submit a request to contact@beestat.io.
